About this tool

Lost and Found Management System

A tool built to streamline lost and found operations across university campuses with elegance and efficiency.

Easy Search

Advanced filtering and intelligent matching algorithms ensure you find your lost items quickly and effortlessly.

Notifications

Instant notifications when items matching your description are reported, keeping you connected at all times.

Data Security

Multi-layer authentication ensures only rightful owners can claim items, maintaining trust and security.

Student Friendly

Seamlessly integrated with university systems for a unified, streamlined experience across all departments.

✦

Our Mission

To create a compassionate, technology-driven ecosystem where lost belongings find their way home, fostering community trust and reducing the stress of losing valuable items on campus.
